Google has added "Study Notebooks" to the Gemini app, a feature designed for test prep and learning new subjects. It functions as a personalized learning space that supports users from lesson creation through quizzes and progress tracking.

## Details

- **How it works**: Specify what you want to learn, and Gemini generates custom lessons tailored to your strengths and weaknesses
- **Bite-sized lessons**: Short, easily digestible lessons tailored to individual learning needs
- **Comprehension checks**: Practice quizzes assess your understanding
- **Progress dashboard**: A custom dashboard visualizes your learning progress and highlights areas that need more focus
- **Who it's for**: Students preparing for exams or anyone looking to efficiently learn a new subject

## How to try it

Available through the Gemini app. There's no explicit mention of regional or plan-based restrictions, and the official blog also includes a video demo of how to use it.
