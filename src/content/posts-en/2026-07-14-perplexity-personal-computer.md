Perplexity has announced "Personal Computer," which extends its cloud-based orchestration feature "Computer" all the way to a user's local machine. It automatically carries out tasks across local files, native apps, and web services.

## Details

- **What it can do**: Organize local files, cross-reference local data with online information, take voice commands, and run continuous task automation across email, messaging, and various apps
- **How it works**: Ask it to "read my to-do list," and it will actually go and complete the items on it
- **How to operate it**: Launch it via a keyboard shortcut on Mac, or with voice commands inside native apps like Notes
- **Availability**: Currently **limited to Perplexity Max subscribers**. A waitlist is available as the rollout expands

## How to try it

- Available for download on Mac at perplexity.ai/personal-computer (priority given to Max subscribers on the waitlist)
- Pricing is included in the existing Max subscription, with no mention of additional fees
