OpenAI has updated its ChatGPT-integrated image generation model to "ChatGPT Images 2.0." As the latest iteration of the image generation feature descended from DALL-E, it brings a particularly large improvement in the accuracy of text rendering across multiple languages.

## Details

- **Accuracy and composition control**: Improved fidelity to specified details and better consistency in complex compositions
- **Multilingual text rendering**: Now supports accurate text generation across a wide range of writing systems, including Japanese, Arabic, Korean, Devanagari, Cyrillic, Bengali, Greek, and Chinese
- **Range of styles**: Improved fidelity across diverse styles, including photorealistic, illustration, comic, pixel art, and editorial design
- **Reflecting current knowledge**: Strengthened ability to translate up-to-date knowledge and contextual understanding into clear visuals
- **Aspect ratios**: Supports variable sizes for generation, from banners to tall mobile-oriented images

## How to try it

Available via "Try in ChatGPT" from the image generation feature within ChatGPT. No additional plan subscription or special setup is required (pricing details were not specified).
