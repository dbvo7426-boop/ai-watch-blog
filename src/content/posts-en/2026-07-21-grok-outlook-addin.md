xAI has announced "Grok for Outlook," a free add-in for Microsoft Outlook. It can summarize long email threads, turn a short note into a polished reply, and keep the inbox organized automatically. This is the fourth Microsoft 365 integration after the existing Word, Excel, and PowerPoint add-ins.

## Details

- **Thread summarization**: Condenses lengthy email conversations, identifying decisions, responsible parties, and pending action items
- **Reply drafting**: Give it brief notes and Grok turns them into a reply that matches how you write, or composes a new message from scratch — tone and recipients can be refined before sending
- **Inbox organization**: Automatically archives completed discussions, filters spam, and prioritizes messages that need a response
- **Additional capabilities**: Reads attachments and can search the web and X without leaving Outlook

## How to try it

- Install it as an add-in from the Microsoft Marketplace
- Available to paid X and SuperGrok subscribers
- Matching add-ins are also available for Word, Excel, and PowerPoint
