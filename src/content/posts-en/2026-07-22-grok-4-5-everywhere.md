xAI announced that Grok 4.5, the model it introduced earlier in July, is now available everywhere: grok.com, X (formerly Twitter), and dedicated iOS and Android apps. This brings xAI's most capable model to mobile devices, not just desktop.

## Details

- **Improved conversation**: Grok 4.5 "follows what you're actually asking, keeps track of longer conversations, and gives clearer, more reliable answers"
- **End-to-end complex tasks**: Users can build out a working spreadsheet, turn an outline into clean slides and diagrams, draft clear prose, and pull the key points from a long PDF
- **Faster reasoning on hard questions**: The model is designed to speed up reasoning on difficult problems
- **Microsoft 365 integration**: Grok is already integrated into Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook through dedicated add-ins

## How to try it

- Available now on grok.com, X, and the iOS/Android apps
- Existing Grok and X apps update automatically to Grok 4.5
