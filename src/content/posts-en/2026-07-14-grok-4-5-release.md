xAI released a new model, "Grok 4.5." Purpose-built for coding, agentic tasks, and knowledge work, it was co-developed with Cursor and trained on large-scale data spanning coding, science, engineering, and mathematics.

## Details

- **Benchmarks**: Achieved a 64.7% resolve rate on SWE Bench Pro, and 29.0% on SWE Marathon — the top score among competitors
- **Token efficiency**: Handles equivalent tasks using roughly 4.2x fewer tokens than Opus 4.8. Processing speed of 80 tokens per second puts it on par with fast models
- **Strengths**: Handles a wide range of tasks, from complex coding work involving Rust and C/C++ to office document creation in Excel, PowerPoint, and Word

## How to try it

- Available immediately via **Grok Build**, **Cursor (all plans)**, and the **xAI API** (console.x.ai)
- A free trial is also available for a limited time on Grok Build and Cursor
- Availability in the EU is expected to be delayed until mid-July
