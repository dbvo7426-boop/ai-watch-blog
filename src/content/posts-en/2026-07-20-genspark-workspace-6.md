Genspark has announced its largest update to date, "AI Workspace 6.0." Built around the concept that "work does itself," the release restructures the entire platform into four layers — memory, intelligence, build suites, and team — marking the biggest overhaul since AI Workspace 4.0.

## Details

- **SecondBrain (memory layer)**: Addresses the problem that "AI today is incredibly capable, but it still has goldfish memory" by consolidating emails, meetings, chats, documents, apps, and Genspark projects into one persistent context
- **SecondBrain Note**: A new AI voice recorder that captures meeting discussions in real time; the recordings are automatically turned into notes and fed into SecondBrain's knowledge base
- **Super Agent (intelligence layer)**: Automatically draws on the context stored in SecondBrain, eliminating the need to re-explain your identity, current projects, and history every time
- **Build Suite (new)**: Adds "Genspark Design," which turns concepts into production-ready designs, prototypes, and videos, and "AgentBase," which builds custom dashboards, CRMs, and internal systems from your existing data; Code carries over from before
- **Office Suite expanded**: Alongside Slides, Sheets, and Docs, a new "GenMail" tool handles email prioritization and drafts replies automatically
- **Content Suite**: Chat, Image, Video, and Music continue to be offered
- **GenTeam (team layer)**: A collaboration layer where humans and AI agents work side by side, with one-click agent deployment and a shared chat interface for teams

## How to try it

- You can start using it immediately at genspark.ai; no specific pricing plan was disclosed
- Where April 2026's "AI Workspace 4.0" focused on unifying desktop automation with Office integration, 6.0 goes further by putting persistent context management — via SecondBrain — at the core of the platform
