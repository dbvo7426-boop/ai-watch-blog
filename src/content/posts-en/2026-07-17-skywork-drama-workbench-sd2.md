Kunlun Wanwei has revamped Tiangong's "Short Drama Workbench," adding an agent-driven intelligent storyboarding feature called "SD2.0" along with an "infinite canvas." The update aims to shift AI short-drama production away from leaving things to random generation and toward a controllable production pipeline, addressing common problems like inconsistent character faces and positioning, and stories that fall apart.

## Details

- **SD2.0 intelligent storyboarding**: A six-stage workflow — script upload, asset extraction, blocking diagram generation, prompt templates, storyboard scripting, and video generation — with human checkpoints for intervention and review at each stage
- **Character consistency**: A Director Agent interprets the script and automatically generates multi-angle assets for characters, scenes, and props. It maps reference compositions across consecutive shots to prevent drift in faces and positioning
- **Infinite canvas**: Consolidates production assets and outputs into a single project view. Supports checking scenes in 720-degree panorama and pre-planning character placement and group scenes on a 3D director's stage
- **Multilingual support**: Native English support means it can be used directly for producing short dramas aimed at overseas markets
- **Track record**: Works produced with this workbench (such as "The Sacrificed Son Rises to God" and "Golden Dragon: Yield to None") reportedly generated "million-dollar" revenue within just seven days of release on the company's own platform, DramaWave
- **What's next**: Plans call for phased integration of multiple video generation models, including Skyreels, Seedance, and Kling

## How to try it

- Studio management features include permission-based sub-accounts and cost tracking by project, team, and time period
- Tiangong AI's short drama business has grown into Kunlun Wanwei's leading revenue driver, outpacing the company's broader AI tools business in annual recurring revenue (ARR); this update is part of that effort to strengthen production capacity
- More details are available on the official Skywork/Tiangong sites (skywork.ai / tiangong.cn)
