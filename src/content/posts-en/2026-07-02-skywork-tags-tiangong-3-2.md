Kunlun Wanwei announced a new feature called "Skywork Tags" alongside the release of "Tiangong 3.2." Rather than positioning it as a personal assistant, the company is presenting a new form of AI agent that can join a team's group chat directly.

## Details

- **How it works**: Simply mentioning "@Skywork" inside a group chat on Slack, Feishu, DingTalk, Discord, Telegram, or similar tools brings the AI agent into the discussion in real time to help solve problems
- **Designed not to disrupt workflows**: There's no need to switch to a different tool — users can summon the AI right within the chat flow they already use
- **Context accumulation**: Through repeated interactions with team members, it gradually learns the team's conversation patterns, project background, and individual preferences, becoming more accurate the more it's used
- **Internal validation results**: A shared team version of the agent reportedly showed higher expertise and accuracy on complex tasks than a personal AI assistant, and the team that tested it fully switched over to the shared version

## How to try it

- Skywork Tags ships as part of the Tiangong 3.2 update, and can be used by inviting or mentioning Skywork within a supported chat tool
- Details on pricing plans, supported languages, and other specific conditions are available on Skywork's official site (skywork.ai)
