Manus has added the ability to directly generate and download native PowerPoint (.pptx) files, rather than converting from a web format. The standout feature is that it outputs files with editable charts and tables intact.

## Details

- **Flexible source inputs**: Presentations can be created from a variety of inputs, including spreadsheet data, research topics, PDFs, URLs, and meeting notes
- **Editing features**: Includes "Elements" (toggle which components are shown) and "Edit Data" (change the underlying figures) for editing charts directly within Manus before downloading
- **Data preservation**: Charts are exported with their underlying data table embedded, so they remain editable in PowerPoint or Google Slides after downloading
- **How it works**: Select "PowerPoint" mode in the Slides tool, describe your topic or upload data, and Manus analyzes the numbers, identifies trends, and builds editable charts

## How to try it

- Currently available in **beta** to users on the 1.6 model and Max model
- Also supports live integration with Google Sheets and can incorporate research findings
