Lindy announced "Lindy Enterprise," a full suite of governance features that lets companies safely deploy and control AI agents across their organizations. Built around the recognition that "rushing to embed AI into existing workflows and systems can become an IT and compliance nightmare," it provides structure, safety, and organizational oversight.

## Details

- **Access and identity management**: Unifies authentication across internal tools with SSO (single sign-on). SCIM provisioning automates adding, updating, and removing users
- **Visibility and reliability**: Provides "Evals," a feature that measures agent performance against quality standards, plus audit logs that record every change, action, and integration
- **Security and governance**: Obtained SOC 2 Type II certification and supports HIPAA and GDPR compliance. Admins can centrally manage agents with locked configurations and credit caps, and permissions can be finely controlled on a per-app basis
- **Background**: Aimed at addressing the corporate challenge of grassroots AI agent adoption moving faster than IT and compliance departments can keep up with

## How to try it

Lindy Enterprise is offered as an add-on enterprise option on top of Lindy's existing plans. Detailed pricing and onboarding are handled through an inquiry channel on the official website.
