xAI has announced "Grok for Excel," a free add-in for Microsoft Excel. Users can select a range and ask Grok what moved, why, and what stands out, and it responds by citing specific cells while also generating charts.

## Details

- **Data analysis**: Select a cell range and ask Grok a question — it explains what changed and what's notable, citing specific cells and generating charts as needed
- **Formula creation**: Converts plain-language instructions (like "total sales by month") into Excel formulas for calculations, sorting, and filling in data
- **Scenario testing**: Run forecasts and downside analysis without manually rebuilding the spreadsheet
- **External connectors**: Can pull in information from email, SharePoint, and Google Drive via connectors
- **Broader rollout**: The same Microsoft Marketplace listing also offers Grok add-ins for Word and PowerPoint

## How to try it

- Install it from the Microsoft Marketplace as a free Microsoft 365 add-in
- No additional subscription cost — included for anyone with a Microsoft 365 plan
