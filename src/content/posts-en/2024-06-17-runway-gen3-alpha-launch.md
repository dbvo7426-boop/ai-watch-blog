Runway has announced its next-generation video generation model, "Gen-3 Alpha." The company says it represents a major advance over Gen-2 in three areas — fidelity, consistency, and motion — highlighting its newfound ability to generate photorealistic human depictions and natural textures.

## Details

- **Improved fidelity**: Substantial improvements to the accuracy of textures, lighting, and photorealistic human depiction
- **Improved consistency**: Better motion consistency throughout an entire clip, resulting in fewer visual breakdowns
- **Improved motion**: Now able to generate physically plausible, highly responsive motion
- **Integration with existing features**: In addition to text-to-video, image-to-video, and text-to-image tools, existing control features such as Motion Brush, Advanced Camera Controls, and Director Mode now support Gen-3 Alpha
- **Availability**: Rolled out within days of the announcement to Runway subscribers, including enterprise customers and Creative Partners Program participants

## What happened next

Gen-3 Alpha brought photorealistic human depiction to a practical level, marking a turning point where video generation AI began to be treated as a genuine filmmaking tool. Building on this technology, the model evolved into "Gen-4" in March 2025, with dramatically improved character and scene consistency.
