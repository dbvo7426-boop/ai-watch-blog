Vibe-coding service Lovable announced it had raised a $330 million Series B round. Its valuation reached $6.6 billion, marking a major further jump in less than half a year since becoming a unicorn in July 2025.

## Details

- **Amount raised and valuation**: Raised $330 million at a $6.6 billion valuation
- **Key investors**: Led by CapitalG and Menlo Ventures' "Anthology" fund. Numerous corporate-affiliated funds participated, including NVIDIA's NVentures, Salesforce Ventures, Databricks Ventures, Deutsche Telekom's T.Capital, Atlassian Ventures, and HubSpot Ventures
- **Other participating investors**: Khosla Ventures, DST Global, EQT Growth, and Kinship Ventures also joined, along with existing investors Accel, Creandum, and Evantic, who invested again
- **Context for the growth**: Represents a major valuation jump in just five months since the July 2025 Series A (at an $1.8 billion valuation)

## How to try it

This is funding news, not a new feature for general users. Lovable's service itself remains available as usual at lovable.dev.
