AI music generation company Suno announced it raised over $400 million in a Series D round led by Bond Capital. The post-money valuation reached $5.4 billion, more than doubling from $2.45 billion in November 2025 in under six months.

## Details

- **Amount raised and valuation**: Raised over $400 million at a post-money valuation of $5.4 billion
- **Key investors**: Led by Bond Capital, with participation from IVP, Forerunner, Union Square Ventures, Alkeon Capital Management, and Quiet. Existing investors Matrix, Lightspeed, Menlo Ventures, and Schroders Capital also added to their stakes
- **Recent valuation trajectory**: Valuation doubled to $5.4 billion in just six months, up from $2.45 billion in November 2025 (when it raised $250 million)
- **Relation to litigation**: The large raise comes amid an ongoing copyright infringement lawsuit brought by major record labels involving roughly 61,000 songs

## How to try it

This is a funding announcement, not a new feature for general users. Suno's music generation service itself remains available as usual at suno.com.
