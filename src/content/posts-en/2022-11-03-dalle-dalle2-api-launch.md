OpenAI released DALL-E 2 as an API, letting developers embed image generation directly into their own apps, websites, and SaaS products. Anyone with an OpenAI API account could start using it as part of a public beta, marking the milestone where DALL-E 2's commercial rollout truly began.

## Details

- **Launch date**: The API launched on November 3, 2022, available to everyone as a public beta
- **Pricing structure**: Tiered by resolution — $0.02 per 1024x1024 image, $0.018 for 512x512, and $0.016 for 256x256
- **Enterprise discounts**: Volume discounts were also made available to high-usage businesses through OpenAI's enterprise team
- **Usage stats**: By this point, more than 3 million people were generating over 4 million images per day, OpenAI reported

## What happened next

The API release led to DALL-E 2 being embedded into a wide range of third-party apps and services, marking a turning point where image generation AI expanded from personal use into business applications. This trend fed directly into DALL-E 3 the following year and later native integration into ChatGPT.
