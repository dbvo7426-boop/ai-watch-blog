Lovable announced a major update, "Lovable 2.0." Its biggest feature is that the chat function itself has become agentic, now able to make autonomous, multi-step decisions while referencing files, checking logs, and querying databases.

## Details

- **Agentic chat**: Introduced "Chat Mode Agent," which reasons across multiple steps and can autonomously search files, check logs, and query databases as needed
- **Multiplayer features**: Added workspace functionality that lets teams collaborate on projects together, with a shared credit pool for Pro (individual) and Teams (team) plans
- **Security scan**: Introduced a new "Security Scan" feature that surfaces vulnerabilities within the app
- **Developer-facing features**: Also added "Dev Mode" for editing code directly, visual editing tools for adjusting appearance, and the ability to purchase and connect custom domains
- **Pricing**: Streamlined pricing around two simple plans, Pro and Teams

## What happened next

Lovable 2.0's richer team and security features helped drive adoption beyond individual developers and into enterprise use. Three months later, in July 2025, the company raised a $200 million Series A at an $1.8 billion valuation, becoming a unicorn and further accelerating its rapid growth.
