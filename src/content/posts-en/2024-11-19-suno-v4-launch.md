Suno announced its new music generation model, "v4." It features clearer audio quality, more intelligible lyrics, and more dynamic song structure than before, and was launched alongside a new "Remaster" feature that lets users upgrade existing songs to v4 quality.

## Details

- **Improved audio and lyrics**: Achieves clearer audio quality and more intelligible lyrics than previous models
- **Evolved song structure**: Supports more dynamic, lively song arrangements
- **Remaster feature**: A new feature that lets users upgrade songs previously generated with earlier versions, such as v3, to v4 quality
- **Timing context**: The announcement came amid ongoing lawsuits from major labels, and coincided with reports that renowned producer Timbaland had joined as a strategic advisor, along with a partnership with Audible Magic and the appointment of a new Chief Product Officer (CPO)

## What happened next

v4 significantly raised the quality of Suno's music generation, further expanding both professional and amateur use. Half a year later, in May 2025, "v4.5" arrived, supporting song generation of up to eight minutes and continuing the pace of feature improvements.
