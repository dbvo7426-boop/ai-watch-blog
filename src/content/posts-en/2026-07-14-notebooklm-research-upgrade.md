Google has significantly upgraded NotebookLM. Powered by the latest Gemini models (Gemini 3.5, Antigravity), it now offers advanced reasoning, code execution, and support for a wide range of output formats.

## Details

- **Code execution**: Can now write and run code in a secure cloud environment, unlocking access to over 100 software skills
- **Improved accuracy**: Recorded a 69.9% win rate on document analysis and a 78.2% win rate on web research
- **Expanded output formats**: Generates content in downloadable, editable formats including PDFs with charts, Excel, PowerPoint, PNG/SVG data visualizations, Markdown, JSON, and CSV
- **Research assistance**: Even before you've organized your sources, NotebookLM can search the web itself and suggest relevant information

## How to try it

- Available to: **Google AI Ultra** subscribers, and Workspace business customers with **AI Ultra** or **AI Expanded Access**
- Rolling out globally starting today
- Get started by uploading a source, or by having NotebookLM suggest relevant material from the web
