xAI announced its new model "Grok-2" as a beta release. The company said it represented a major leap forward from the previous model, Grok-1.5, in chat, coding, and reasoning, and it features a design that combines newly added visual understanding with real-time access to information from X (formerly Twitter).

## Details

- **From announcement to availability**: Beta access launched on X six days after the August 13 announcement, on August 20
- **Performance**: Positioned as having "frontier-level" capability in chat, coding, and reasoning
- **Visual understanding**: Newly added the ability to understand images
- **Availability**: Initially offered as beta access to X Premium and Premium+ subscribers
- **Enterprise rollout**: That December, Grok-2 and Grok-2 mini also became available as enterprise APIs

## What happened next

Grok-2 marked a milestone showing xAI's transformation from a company that shipped a single model into one continuously developing frontier models. Half a year later, in February 2025, Grok-3 — trained with reportedly ten times the compute of the previous generation — was announced, further intensifying xAI's development race.
