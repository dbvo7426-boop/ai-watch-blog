Suno released its new model, "v5.5." Living up to the company's description of it as "our most personal model," it simultaneously launched three new features — "Voices," "Custom Models," and "My Taste" — that let users create music reflecting their own voice and preferences.

## Details

- **Voices**: A feature that captures your own singing voice, letting you hear a version of yourself in Suno's songs. Registered through an identity-verification process that matches against random phrases, ensuring it can't be used by anyone else
- **Custom Models**: A feature that lets you upload your own songs made outside Suno to tune a personal model closer to your own style. Pro and Premier users can create up to 3 custom models
- **My Taste**: A feature available to all users that personalizes recommendations based on frequently used genres and preferred moods
- **Audio quality**: Delivers richer arrangements, clearer vocals, and more dynamic sound across every genre

## How to try it

- Available at suno.com; identity verification for Voices and uploads for Custom Models can both be done from each user's account settings
- Some features, such as the cap on the number of Custom Models you can create, are limited by plan, and require a Pro or Premier subscription
