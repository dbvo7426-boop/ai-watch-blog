Midjourney released "niji," an anime- and illustration-focused image generation model co-developed with animation studio Spellbrush. Tuned separately from the standard Midjourney model, it was designed to more accurately reproduce 2D-style line art and anime-inspired composition and color.

## Details

- **Development**: Co-developed with Spellbrush, using data and tuning specialized for anime and illustration
- **Closed beta**: Sign-ups for a closed beta began in November 2022, with access granted to a limited group of users before the official release
- **How to use it**: Accessible from within the regular Midjourney Discord server by adding the `--niji` parameter
- **Strengths**: Earned high praise for 2D-style depictions — like faces, eyes, and hair — that the standard model tended to struggle with

## What happened next

niji went on to evolve through its own version line — "niji 5," "niji 6," and "niji 7" — updated on a separate schedule from the main Midjourney product, becoming one of its major product lines. It also led to the launch of the dedicated "niji · journey" app, marking Midjourney's first major move signaling its commitment to the anime and illustration market.
