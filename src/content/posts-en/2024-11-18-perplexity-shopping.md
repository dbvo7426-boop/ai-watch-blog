On November 18, 2024, Perplexity announced an AI-powered conversational shopping feature for U.S. Pro members. Ask about a product in natural language, and it presents product cards including price, seller, features, and pros and cons — and with payment information saved, purchases can be completed in a single click. Coming right before the holiday shopping season, the announcement expanded Perplexity's reach from search into e-commerce.

## Details

- **Search experience**: Handles even vague questions like "what's the best winter jacket for commuting by ferry in San Francisco?" by picking up on the implied requirements
- **Personalization**: Learns from past search history and preference patterns (such as design style), aiming to tailor recommendations to the user's taste rather than advertisers' interests
- **Technical integration**: Pulls product information through integrations with e-commerce platforms like Shopify stores
- **One-click purchasing**: For supported products and sellers, checkout can be completed entirely within Perplexity. Pro members get free shipping
- **Revenue model**: At launch, no sponsored placements or affiliate commissions were introduced, with the company emphasizing ad-free, neutral recommendations. The rollout started with desktop and web, with iOS/Android to follow a few weeks later

## What happened next

The shopping feature represented Perplexity's search for a revenue stream distinct from search advertising, and it later led to partnerships with major retailers including Amazon and expanded checkout experiences like "Buy with Pro." The announcement was an early sign of the AI agent-driven automated purchasing race that would intensify from 2025 onward.
