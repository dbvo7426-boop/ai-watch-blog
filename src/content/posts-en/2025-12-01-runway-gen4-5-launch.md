Runway announced its latest video generation model, "Gen-4.5." It says the model scored an Elo of 1,247 on Artificial Analysis's published text-to-video benchmark, taking first place ahead of models from Google and OpenAI.

## Details

- **Benchmark results**: Ranked first in the world on the Artificial Analysis Text to Video benchmark, with an Elo score of 1,247
- **Physical representation**: Achieves more realistic physics simulation, including object momentum and liquid behavior
- **Detail consistency**: Fine details such as individual strands of hair and material textures now hold together better through motion
- **Range of styles**: Supports a wide range of styles, from photorealism to stylized animation
- **Character expression**: Improved emotional expression and facial nuance enable more natural character performances

## How to try it

- Gen-4.5 is available at no extra cost on all existing Runway paid subscription plans
- Existing control modes such as Image to Video, Keyframes, and Video to Video are being rolled out to support Gen-4.5 as well
- Enterprises can also inquire about custom solutions
