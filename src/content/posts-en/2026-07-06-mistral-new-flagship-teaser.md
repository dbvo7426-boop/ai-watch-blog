On July 6, 2026, Mistral AI CEO Arthur Mensch teased a new open-weight flagship model. He described it as a new line of "fat but sparse" Mixture-of-Experts models, with early access for research institutions, government agencies, and industry partners set to begin sometime in July.

## Details

- **Architecture**: Reported to be a MoE model expected to significantly exceed the scale of the current flagship, "Mistral Large 3" (675 billion total parameters)
- **Model name**: No official name has been announced. Mensch only jokingly shot down "Le Chaton Fat" as a candidate name, without revealing the actual one
- **Performance and benchmarks**: No specific figures have been disclosed. The model is positioned as an effort to close the "frontier gap" with leading closed API-based models
- **License**: Not yet confirmed, but early access is expected to be limited to research, government, and industry partners
- **Timing**: Early access for partners is set to begin sometime in July, with general availability planned for "summer 2026." The exact general availability date has not been set

## How to try it

- The model is currently pre-release, with early access limited to research institutions, government agencies, and industry partners
- Details such as the general availability date, official name, benchmarks, and licensing terms are still pending future announcements
