xAI has added an "Automations" feature to Grok. Pitched as "write what you need done once, then just choose when it runs — Grok handles the rest," it lets Grok autonomously handle routine tasks triggered by a schedule or an incoming email.

## Details

- **How to set it up**: Write your instructions as if composing a chat message, attach any files, connectors, or skills, and save it to register it as an Automation
- **Trigger types**: Two kinds — "scheduled" (runs at any recurring frequency and time, such as daily, weekly, or monthly) and "email-triggered" (runs when an email matching conditions like sender, recipient, or subject is received)
- **Reviewing results**: Each run appears as its own conversation that Grok completes and saves to a run history, which you can revisit later or continue chatting within
- **Notification options**: Choose from email, app notification, both, or no notifications
- **Ways to create one**: Either ask directly in chat, or choose from ready-made templates on the Automations page

## How to try it

- Available at grok.com and on the iOS/Android apps
- Scheduled triggers are available to all users. Email triggers require a **SuperGrok** subscription
