Kunlun Tech announced the official free release of the o1 and 4o versions of its large language model "Tiangong 4.0" on the Tiangong website and app. This opened up to general users a model that had been in invitation-only testing the previous month.

## Details

- **Skywork o1**: Described as China's first o1-class model with Chinese-language logical reasoning capability, handling a wide range of reasoning tasks including math, coding, logic, common sense, and ethical judgment. Released simultaneously in both an open-source version and a higher-performance dedicated version
- **Skywork 4o**: The multimodal model powering the real-time voice assistant "Skyo," delivering natural conversation with emotional expression, fast responses, and multilingual switching
- **Technical updates**: A major overhaul including optimization of the process reward model (PRM), improvements to the reasoning system based on the Q* algorithm, and the introduction of a proprietary Step-DAPO algorithm
- **Availability**: Available for free on both the Tiangong website and app

## What happened next

The free release of "Tiangong 4.0" marked a milestone in Kunlun Tech's large language model development. The company went on to expand into image, music, and video generation models, eventually leading to the December 2025 announcement of "Skywork," an integrated workspace combining document creation, video summarization, and podcast production.
