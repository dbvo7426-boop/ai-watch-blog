Google has published a how-to piece on using the camera within the Gemini app's Live feature to get real-time help with whatever is in front of you — from fixing a broken appliance to tidying a room to getting shopping suggestions, just by pointing your lens at it. This is a usage guide for an existing feature rather than a new product announcement.

## Details

- **Basic usage**: In the Gemini app, tap the Live icon, then tap the camera icon to start Gemini Live. From there, just point the lens at whatever you need help with
- **Key use cases**:
  - Getting step-by-step repair instructions for something broken or malfunctioning
  - Getting tips for tidying up a space
  - Sharing your screen for help navigating an app
  - Brainstorming design ideas or getting personalized shopping recommendations
- **Other supported scenarios**: Troubleshooting error codes, deciphering instruction manuals, and identifying unknown objects
- **Availability**: The article assumes usage within the Gemini app but doesn't specify supported OS, regional availability, or subscription requirements

## How to try it

- Open the Gemini app, tap the Live icon, then the camera icon to start
- Point the camera at whatever you need help with and start talking for real-time assistance
- See Google's official blog post for further availability details
