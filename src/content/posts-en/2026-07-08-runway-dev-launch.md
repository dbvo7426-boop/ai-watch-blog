Runway unveiled "Runway Dev," an AI media platform for developers. Its defining feature is that it lets developers call multiple frontier models — spanning image, video, audio, and real-time characters — from a single unified API.

## Details

- **Unified API**: Provides access to flagship models such as Gen-4.5, Aleph 2.0, and Act-Two through a single API
- **Core components**: Built around three pillars — "Recipes," ready-made setups for specific use cases; "Workflows," for chaining together multi-step processes; and "Characters," for real-time voice-enabled interaction
- **Enterprise readiness**: Emphasizes security and reliability for business use, including SOC 2 Type II compliance, a no-training-on-your-data commitment, and a 99.9% uptime guarantee
- **Existing adopters**: Companies including Adobe, ElevenLabs, Shutterstock, Figma Weave, Gamma, and Silverside are said to already be using it to generate millions of videos, images, and other creative content

## How to try it

- Accessible now at dev.runwayml.com, already available
- Specific pricing had not been published as of the announcement; check the official site for details
- Positioned for professional developers and enterprise teams, offered as a separate platform from the consumer-facing Runway app
