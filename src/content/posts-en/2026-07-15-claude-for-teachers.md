Anthropic has unveiled "Claude for Teachers," a dedicated plan for K-12 educators in the United States. Paired with materials aligned to learning standards across all 50 states, it brings Claude's premium features to the classroom.

## Details

- **Lesson planning**: Build lessons around high-quality materials tied to state learning standards
- **Personalization for students**: Create personalized materials for students at different proficiency levels
- **Data analysis**: Analyze class learning data and apply the insights to teaching strategy
- **Automating routine tasks**: Schedule recurring tasks such as "check exit tickets every day"
- **Integrated tools**: Integrates with nine education tools: ASSISTments, Brisk Teaching, Canva Education, Coteach, Diffit, Eedi, MagicSchool, Snorkl, and TeachFX
- **Training**: An "AI Fluency" training program co-developed with Teach for America and the American Federation of Teachers (AFT) is also available
- **Privacy**: Data is not used for model training, and terms of use compliant with FERPA were developed with guidance from the AFT

## How to try it

- Available only to **verified K-12 educators** in the US (must be 18 or older, per Claude's age requirements)
- Sign up by June 30, 2027 for **completely free** access for one year
- Dedicated plans for schools and districts are planned for the future
