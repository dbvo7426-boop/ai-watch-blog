On July 19, 2026, Alibaba unveiled a preview of "Qwen3.8-Max," the new flagship model in its Qwen lineup, via the Qwen team's official X (formerly Twitter) account. With roughly 2.4 trillion parameters, Alibaba describes it as "one of the most powerful models currently available, on par with frontier-class models and second only to Fable 5." The company also indicated it plans to release the model with open weights in the near future.

## Details

- **Parameter scale**: 2.4 trillion parameters. Described as a model that is "still continuously evolving," suggesting training is ongoing even now
- **Performance claims**: Alibaba claims it is "on par with frontier-class AI models, second only to Fable 5." No detailed benchmark figures were disclosed in this announcement
- **Availability**: The preview is already accessible through Alibaba Cloud's Token Plan and the coding platforms "Qoder" and "QoderWork"
- **Promise of open weights**: While currently offered as a closed preview, Alibaba has explicitly stated it plans to release the weights soon — a potential shift away from the "flagship models stay closed-API-only" approach that has held since the Qwen3.7 generation
- **Where it fits in the series**: The latest entry in a rapid string of flagship releases, following Qwen2.5-Max (January 2025), Qwen3 (April 2025), Qwen3.6, and Qwen3.7-Max (May 2026)

## How to try it

- Developers can access the Qwen3.8-Max preview right now via Alibaba Cloud's Token Plan, through the coding platforms "Qoder" and "QoderWork"
- Consumer availability and third-party benchmark verification have not yet taken place; further updates are expected, including on the timing of the open-weight release
