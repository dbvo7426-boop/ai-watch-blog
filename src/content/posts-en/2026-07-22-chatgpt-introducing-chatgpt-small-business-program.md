OpenAI has announced ChatGPT for Small Business, a new program designed to help small business owners use AI as a "force multiplier." Centered on ChatGPT Work, an agent powered by GPT-5.6, the program provides free training and educational content, plus partnerships with other companies, to help embed AI into everyday work like accounting, marketing, and e-commerce.

## Details

- **Virtual training**: Product-specific webinars covering how to apply ChatGPT Work across business functions such as accounting, marketing, and e-commerce, along with partner sessions and Q&As
- **In-person academies**: Hands-on local events across the US where business owners can work through practical exercises alongside peers. OpenAI cites strong results from past participants: 78% built a functional AI workflow in a single day, and 42% saved more than five hours a week
- **Educational resources**: Customer stories, interactive guides compatible with ChatGPT Work, and short-form videos featuring practical prompts
- **Partner ecosystem**: Integrations with companies including Shopify, Intuit, Slack, Dropbox, and Wix, along with exclusive promotional offers
- **Core technology**: The program is built around ChatGPT Work, a GPT-5.6-powered agent capable of completing multi-step tasks and integrating with business files and applications

## How to try it

- Small business owners can sign up at openai.com/leads/small-business/ to receive updates and event information
- Specific pricing details and a detailed rollout schedule were not disclosed at the time of writing, so more information is expected to follow
