AI agent "Manus" has added a new feature called "Auto-Publish." Every time a build succeeds, the change is now automatically reflected at the live published URL, eliminating the need for manual publishing.

## Details

- **How it works**: Turning on the toggle in the publish menu makes changes go live at the published URL as soon as a build completes (off by default)
- **Supported platforms**: Available on Web, iOS, and Android
- **Safety**: If a build fails, the published site is unaffected
- **Handling multiple changes**: Multiple changes can be queued and processed in order, with support for canceling a deployment in progress
- **Intended use**: Aimed at users in an iterative development phase, making small design tweaks and polish once the overall direction is settled

## How to try it

Available immediately on Web, iOS, and Android. Just turn on the toggle in the publish popover to enable it.
