Anthropic has announced "Claude for Creative Work," a new initiative aimed at creative professionals. Targeting creators across music production, 3D design, VFX, architecture, and design, it ships a new set of connectors linking Claude to nine creative software tools, including Adobe Creative Cloud, plus a new product called Claude Design for rapid idea visualization.

## Details

- **Positioning**: Anthropic states that "Claude can't replace taste or imagination, but it can open up new ways of working," framing the initiative around boosting productivity and creative range for professionals
- **Key capabilities**:
  - On-demand tutoring for learning and mastering creative software
  - Extending software with custom code — scripts, plugins, and shaders
  - Bridging multiple applications across a creative pipeline
  - Rapid idea exploration and visualization through the new Claude Design product
  - Automating repetitive production tasks
- **New connectors (nine total)**: Ableton, Adobe Creative Cloud (covering 50+ tools), Affinity by Canva, Autodesk Fusion, Blender, Resolume Arena/Wire, SketchUp, and Splice
- **Education partnerships**: Anthropic partnered with three institutions — Rhode Island School of Design, Ringling College of Art and Design, and Goldsmiths, University of London
- **Under the hood**: The connectors are built on MCP (Model Context Protocol), and Claude Code can generate scripts or custom integrations to extend them further
- **Pricing/availability**: The announcement does not specify a particular pricing tier
