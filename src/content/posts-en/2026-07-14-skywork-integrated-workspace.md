Skywork.ai has announced "Skywork," which brings together multiple specialized AI agents into a single environment. It stands out for letting users handle document creation, slides, data visualization, website building, video summarization, and podcast production all in one consistent flow without switching apps.

## Details

- **Continuity of context**: Context carries over across different work formats (documents, slides, video, and more), so there's no need to switch between apps
- **Key features**:
  - Real-time collaborative editing and multilingual support
  - One-click website publishing (with a visual editor and database support)
  - "YouVibe," a feature that summarizes YouTube videos into notes and mind maps
  - AI-generated podcasts produced automatically from documents
  - Verified data search across finance, technology, and business domains
- **Target users**: Creators, analysts, and educators who want to streamline everything from ideation to execution

## How to try it

The press release does not mention a specific launch date or pricing. Details are available on Skywork's official site (skywork.ai).
